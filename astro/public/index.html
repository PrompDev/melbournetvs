Clear trade boundaries

The right person for the right work.

  • Standard mounting is scoped separately from fixed electrical work.
  • New or relocated power requires a Victorian licensed electrician.
  • Regulated communications cabling requires an appropriately registered cabler.
  • The proposed installer and business details are disclosed before booking.
Common questions

Before you request a quote.

How much does TV mounting cost in Melbourne?

The written price depends on the TV, bracket, wall construction, cable plan, access and lift requirements. Send photos so the proposed scope reflects the actual job.

Do you promise same-day installation?

No. During launch we first confirm a suitable local installer, the scope and their actual availability. The quote includes the appointment options available.

Who will come to my home?

Melbourne TVs coordinates the enquiry with an independent installer. Their identity and business details are disclosed before you accept the booking.

Can a new power point be installed behind the TV?

Only a Victorian licensed electrician can complete new or relocated fixed electrical work. It is identified as a separate licensed trade item when required.
