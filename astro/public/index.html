Clear trade boundaries

Power and regulated cabling need the right person.

  • Standard mounting is scoped separately from fixed electrical work.
  • New or relocated power requires a Victorian licensed electrician.
  • Regulated communications cabling requires an appropriately registered cabler.
  • The assigned installer and their business details are disclosed before the appointment.

Common questions

Before you request a quote.

How much does TV mounting cost in Melbourne?

The price depends on the TV, bracket, wall construction, cable plan, access and lift requirements. Send photos and we will return a written scope instead of advertising a price that may not fit the job.

Do you offer same-day installation?

We do not promise same-day work during launch. We first confirm a suitable local installer, the job scope and their availability. Your written quote will include the appointment options actually available.

Who will come to my home?

Melbourne TVs coordinates the enquiry with an independent installer. Their identity and business details are disclosed before you accept the booking, and the commercial arrangement is confirmed in writing.

Can an installer put a new power point behind the TV?

Only a Victorian licensed electrician can complete new or relocated fixed electrical work. If your photos show that this is required, it is identified as a separate licensed trade item.

Which suburbs are available?

All 534 metropolitan localities are in our routing map, but zones open progressively. Send your address and we will confirm whether a primary and backup installer are ready for your area.
