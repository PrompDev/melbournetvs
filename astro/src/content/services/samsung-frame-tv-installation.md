## A Frame TV needs a finish plan

The screen is designed to sit close to the wall, so wall flatness, template placement and the cable exit point matter more than on a conventional mount. The One Connect box also needs ventilation and practical access for future device changes.

Send the model, wall photos, nearby cabinet and outlet positions. The installer can then confirm the bracket, cable path and any separate licensed trade work before booking.
